# Validation Checklist — 3.2.0-alpha.1

1. Open Unity 6000.5.2f1 and wait for compilation.
2. Run `Arcane Engine > 3.2 > Validate Arsenal & Economy`.
3. Load a 3.1 character and verify migration.
4. Open the Arsenal station or on-screen Arsenal button.
5. Drag equipment in the backpack and shared stash.
6. Verify right-click equip and Shift-click transfer.
7. Fill the backpack and confirm further pickups remain on the ground.
8. Test all available crafting operations; invalid operations must not spend currency.
9. Test fracture, corruption, map crafting, and map corruption.
10. Test vendor buy, sell, salvage, refresh, and gambling.
11. Edit and save loot-filter rules.
12. Save, exit, reload, and verify all positions and account data.
13. Confirm no red Console errors.
