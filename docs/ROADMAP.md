# Arcane Engine Roadmap

## 3.0.0-alpha.1 — Persistent map foundation

- Persistent profiles and Level 0 start.
- Mage, Ranger, and Warrior.
- Refuge and map device.
- Forty-tier data model and initial White-map loop.
- Map completion, Mastery, sustain, death, and recovery.
- Minimal item, currency, Constellation, and Ascendancy foundations.
- Save migration and diagnostics.

## 3.0.0-alpha.2 — Itemization and crafting

- Full base-item catalogue.
- Affix pools, tiers, weights, and conflicts.
- Currency operations and crafting station.
- Item comparison and loot filtering.
- Map crafting.

## 3.0.0-alpha.3 — Classes and Ascendancies

- Complete class resources and defenses.
- Nine playable Ascendancies.
- Progression encounters and class starting Cores.

## 3.0.0-alpha.4 — Constellations

- Full modular allocation.
- Attunement and visible conflicts.
- Discovery, respecialization, and initial complete library.

## 3.0.0-alpha.5 — Full Atlas progression

- White, Blue, Yellow, and Red regions.
- Regional bosses, map affixes, target farming, special maps, and pinnacle access.

## 3.0.0-beta.1 — Integrated endgame-first ARPG

- Integrated balance and economy.
- Pinnacle encounters.
- Fracture Run reintegration.
- Performance and accessibility validation.

## Product guardrails

- No campaign in the current program.
- Tier 0 remains permanently recoverable.
- Progression is not entirely random.
- Forty tiers do not scale through health alone.
- SpellForge remains the central identity.
- Classes shape builds without hard-locking creative SpellForge combinations.
