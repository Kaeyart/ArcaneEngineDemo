# Arcane Engine v1.2.4 — Global Facing Test

1. Press Play and choose `Enter Home Base`.
2. Without starting a run, circle the player with the mouse. The casting focus and cyan ground reticle must follow through all 360 degrees.
3. Hold W while aiming behind the player, then hold A while aiming right. Movement must not alter facing.
4. Place the mouse above, below, left, and right of the player and leave it still for three seconds at each position.
5. Move the mouse slowly across the player center. The small dead zone must preserve the previous direction without snapping.
6. Open and close a Home Base station. Aiming must pause for the menu and resume when it closes.
7. Start a dungeon run and repeat steps 2–5.
8. Confirm Left Mouse, Right Mouse, and Q still cast only during an active run.
9. Rotate the camera with Middle Mouse and verify all four directions again.
10. Confirm the Console contains no aim, input, null-reference, or GUILayout errors.
