# Arcane Engine v0.5.0 acceptance checklist

## Clean import

- Close Unity before installation.
- Install over the existing project with the provided command.
- Open `/home/kaey/Desktop/ArcaneEngineDemo_v0.2.0_MEGA_UPDATE/ArcaneEngineDemo` in Unity 6000.5.2f1.
- Confirm the Console has no red compiler errors and shows the v0.5 validation message.
- Confirm the old profile, Essence, Legendary Shards, stored spells, and equipment remain present.

## Home Base and start flow

- Walk around Home Base with WASD; verify cover stops movement.
- Use every physical station with the configured Interact key.
- Start and complete the Apprentice Trial once; return and confirm completion survives restarting Play Mode.
- At the Dungeon Gate, verify Standard, Daily, Custom Seed, and locked/unlocked Endless behavior.
- Select starting spells and confirm duplicate copies and the one-Legendary rule.

## Aim and combat

- Move the mouse across the full window; crosshair, ground reticle, facing, and projectile direction must agree.
- Rotate the camera with middle mouse and repeat the aim test.
- Hit normal, armored, elite, and status-affected enemies; verify one synchronized bar per enemy.
- Confirm Chargers, Leeches, Assassins, Controllers, Mirrors, Summoners, Volatile, and Vampiric enemies show their distinct behavior.
- Break a room prop with a spell and verify physical collision with cover.

## Objectives and room loop

- Clear enough rooms to observe timed, defense, portal/ritual, wave, escort, hold, marked-target, hidden-target, collapse, ordered, and flawless objectives.
- Verify the objective card and progress change from runtime state.
- After combat, choose one center reward, optionally use the reroll shrine, then enter one of three labeled north doors.
- Visit a physical Shop, Safe Room, and Healing Room; verify purchases/services and a single-use font.

## Building and loot

- Open Tab and I during combat, after combat, at rewards, and at route doors.
- Hold Interact on a modifier and confirm it installs on the most recently cast spell when a legal connector exists.
- Tap/hold Item loot, compare it, equip it, mark favorite/junk, and Sell All Junk.
- Raise the loot filter and verify filtered low-rarity items sell automatically while Unique loot never does.
- Equip two/three Warden, Storm, or Ember pieces and verify the set summary and player stats change.

## Progression, boss, and saves

- Reach Run Level 2, choose one physical perk, Save & Quit, Continue, and verify the perk and run state remain.
- Break the boss ward pillars, observe all three phase rules, defeat the boss, take the Unique drop and room reward, then extract.
- Verify the Legendary Shard and Essence remain after returning to Home Base.
- Verify Endless unlocks, bosses occur every five rooms, and an extraction door appears after an Endless boss.
- End a run and verify the recap statistics.
- Check Master/Music/Effects/Ambience/UI sliders, auto-collection settings, key rebinding, resolution, and fullscreen persistence.
