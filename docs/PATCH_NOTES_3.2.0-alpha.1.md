# Arcane Engine 3.2.0-alpha.1 — Arsenal & Economy

This update introduces the first complete persistent loot economy for Arcane Engine.

## Major systems

- 12×5 variable-size character backpack.
- 12×12 shared equipment stash tabs and a dedicated Recovery tab.
- Dedicated Currency, Maps, Spell Cores, and Support Runes storage.
- Mouse drag-and-drop, right-click equip, and Shift-click transfer.
- 110 new equipment bases in addition to the existing catalogue.
- 252 new six-tier affixes in addition to existing modifiers.
- 24 rule-changing Unique items.
- Exceptional items with elevated and sealed affixes.
- 15 distinct crafting currencies.
- Quality, fractured modifiers, sealed modifiers, craft history, and corruption.
- 36 map modifiers and map danger/reward display.
- Map rarity crafting and irreversible map corruption.
- Account-wide Gold, shared stash, vendor stock, salvage, selling, and gambling.
- Ordered loot-filter rules.
- Advanced character-stat display.
- Automatic 3.1 migration with deterministic placement and a Recovery tab.
- Physical Arsenal & Exchange station in the Astral Refuge.

Items are never deleted when placement fails. Failed pickups remain on the ground.
Migration overflow is redirected to Recovery.

Package/source validation passed. Unity compilation and Play Mode require target-editor confirmation.
